3D printing request form

Things to note before submitting

Scaling

You need to scale your models accordingly. Your largest dimensions should be no greater than 200mm (about 7.87 in).
If you are unsure of your model's scale, please use this tool to verify or modify your model's dimensions. You may also use the tool to modify your model.

Naming

Naming your model a unique name is crucial for automation to take its course. Once you are confident your model is properly scaled, name it something unique such as : 98657.stl or " a brief description of your project".stl. Avoid name like thing.stl or project.stl. 
ALL FILES MUST BE .STL FORMAT

Sizing

If your model exceeds 25MB, you will be prompted with an error. To fix it, use this to reduce your file to less than 25MB. Note: reducing your file size will have no effect on your final result or final scale. You are simply reducing the quality of your file. Since 3D-Printers struggle with detail, this will have no effect on final results.
